# NeoOptimize Open-Core Product Model

NeoOptimize will use an open-core model.

## Community Free

Local optimizer, basic health checks, local-first AI guidance, and transparent safety documentation.

## Pro Personal

AI Doctor, scheduled maintenance, update assistant, richer reports, and safer one-click workflows for individual users.

## RMM Team / Business

Fleet dashboard, signed command dispatch, telemetry, Safety Auto-Response, update distribution, and endpoint policy control.

## Enterprise Self-Hosted

Private deployment, custom branding, compliance controls, support workflow, and advanced security guardrails.
